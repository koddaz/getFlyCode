ABOUT

.getFlyCode is a one-man company focused on developing innovative digital solutions for people living with diabetes. With a strong commitment to improving everyday health management, .getFlyCode creates intuitive applications designed to make tracking and understanding glucose levels simpler, more efficient, and less stressful.

By combining thoughtful design with practical features, the goal is to empower users to take control of their diabetes with confidence and ease—whether they're newly diagnosed or managing it long-term. At its core, .getFlyCode believes that managing your health shouldn't be complicated—it should be seamless, accessible, and tailored to your needs.
